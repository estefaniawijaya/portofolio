WELCOME

I'm an undergraduate student at BINUS University majoring in Visual and Communication Design. My interest in art started at a young age and it made me want to pursue it even further. There's still yet a lot to learn and I'm eager to seek new experiences along the way!

Pokemon Trading Card Game
Colourful yet cute take on the original Pokemon Trading Card Game. This project was a university assignment to recreate a card game with our own style.
Sunny Adventure
“Sunny Adventure” is a 2D platform game. This game's goal is to collect stars through obstacles and bringing them back to the star gate.
Kenari Cakery
“Kenari Cakery” is a local cakery famous for their Lapis Surabaya. This project was created to rebrand the logo and packaging towards a modern look.
Bohopanna
“Bohopanna” is a child's clothes shop targeted for young moms who want their kids to look stylish. This project was created to redesign their packaging for new arrivals.
Menantea x Erika Richardo
This is a university project about F&B Ad where I explored special effects in Photoshop. Menantea is a brand that is well known for their fruit teas. So, I chose to highlight fresh fruits with special effects while splashing out of the beverage.
Mrs. Wong
Client requested a chinoiserie themed design for this project. So I combined chinoiserie patterns with a tiger illustration to symbolize the 2022 chinese new year animal. I was trusted to make social media content (Instagram feeds, stories, and reels) and the packaging.
